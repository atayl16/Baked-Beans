Data Points

To allow us to record beans and data about them we have make a list of data points that we will use when analysing a tin. This should stop us from bias towards name brands.

Brand, Shop, Price at purchase, weight of full can, weight of empty can, weight of beans, percentage of beans to liquid, bean count, taste rating, ingredients listed, sauce viscosity, pull ring tin?,length of best before date and Finaly Pictures of the tin & contents.

If you think we have missed an important data point please to Contact Us!
